# Rabbi Character Behavior Rules

The rabbi is the user’s guide. His behavior should feel consistent, warm, and animated.

## Correct Answer
- Switch to celebrating pose (arms up).
- Add a small bounce.
- Optional line: “כל הכבוד!” (“Kol hakavod!”)

## Incorrect Answer
- Switch to arms-crossed or thinking pose.
- Slow head shake or eyebrow raise.
- Optional line: “לא בדיוק… בוא ננסה שוב.”

## Greeting Moments
- Point upward as greeting text appears.
- Slight lean or head tilt.

## Teaching Moments
- Use pointing or teaching pose.
- Gesture toward UI elements (Learn, Review, Library).

## Idle Behavior
Cycle through:
1. Hands-behind-back with blinking.
2. Thinking pose with beard stroke.
3. Yawning/stretching pose.
4. **Exercises screen only:**  
   Rabbi sits in a chair behind him, head resting on hand, eyes drooping as he nods off.

## Emotional Range
- Encouraging, never harsh.
- Curious, thoughtful, celebratory, sleepy.
- Always approachable and friendly.
