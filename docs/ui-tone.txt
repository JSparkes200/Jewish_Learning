# UI Tone & Writing Style Guide

This project uses a warm, Torah-inspired tone that blends clarity with personality.

## Core Tone
- Friendly, encouraging, and wise.
- Feels like a patient rabbi guiding a student.
- Never condescending or overly formal.
- Light humor is welcome.

## Hebrew Usage
Use simple Hebrew phrases sparingly:
- “שלום!”
- “כל הכבוד!”
- “בוא ננסה שוב.”
- “ברוכים הבאים!”

## Writing Style
- Short, clear sentences.
- Avoid jargon.
- Use warm, human phrasing.
- Keep instructions simple and direct.

## Examples
- “Tap a lesson to begin.”
- “Ready to learn something new?”
- “Let’s review what you’ve mastered.”
- “Take your time — I’m right here with you.”

## Avoid
- Corporate or robotic tone.
- Overly long explanations.
- Harsh or discouraging language.
